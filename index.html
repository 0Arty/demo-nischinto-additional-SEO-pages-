About Us

We Offer Dental Implant Solutions
in Hyattsville, MD

We provide a full range of dental implant treatments designed to replace missing teeth with strength, stability, and natural appearance.

Whether you are missing one tooth or an entire arch, our implant solutions are customized to your oral health, bone structure, and long-term goals.

Single Tooth Dental Implants are the ideal solution for replacing one missing tooth without affecting neighboring teeth.

A titanium implant is placed into the jawbone and topped with a custom crown that looks, feels, and functions like a natural tooth.

Multiple Tooth Implants and Implant Bridges replace several missing teeth using strategically placed implants to support a fixed bridge. This option restores chewing ability and prevents bone loss while avoiding removable partial dentures.

Full-Arch and Full-Mouth Dental Implants offer a life-changing solution for patients missing most or all of their teeth. Using advanced techniques, a full arch of teeth can be securely supported by dental implants, providing a permanent, stable alternative to traditional dentures.

Implant-Supported Dentures combine the affordability of dentures with the stability of implants.

Dentures are anchored to implants for improved comfort, better function, and confidence when eating or speaking.

Immediate and Same-Day Implant Options may be available for qualified patients, allowing implant placement and temporary teeth on the same day, reducing treatment time and restoring your smile faster.
